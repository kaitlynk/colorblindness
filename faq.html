Welcome to the Color Blindness Application! On this site, you can find our two projects for PSYCH 3420/6420 - Human Perception: Applications to Computer Graphics, Art, and Visual Display, a Cornell University course taught by Professor David J Field.

Our projects are focused around how to create efficient, useful, and smart designs for websites. We took a two pronged approach and created both a web designer's style guide and a web color applet.

Please feel free to explore the area!
